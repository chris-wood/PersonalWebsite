Professional Experience

    Security and Privacy Researcher, June 2013 - September 2013

    I spent a summer researching security and privacy aspects related to content-centric network (CCN), one of the mainstream proposals for the future Internet architecture. Part of my research involved securing content that is distributed throughout a CCN mesh (data confidentiality), and another involved hiding the link between content names and the actual content with which it is associated (name privacy). See the CCNx project for more details about this upcoming technology.
